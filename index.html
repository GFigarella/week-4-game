Crystals Collector

You will me given a random number at the start of the game

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the number displayed in the box above the crystals. You lose if the total score goes past the number.

The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on them.

These values will change each time the game starts.
